# 07 — Intake Routing Logic

## Lead tags
- hot: asked for times/quote now
- warm: interested, not ready today
- cold: no response after full sequence

## Routing
- hot => owner/closer within 5 minutes
- warm => nurture queue + weekly follow-up
- cold => reactivation list (30+ days)

## KPI priorities
1. Contact rate
2. Booking rate
3. No-show recovery rate
4. Reactivation conversion
