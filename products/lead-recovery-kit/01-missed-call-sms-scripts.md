# 01 — Missed-Call SMS Scripts

Use these when someone calls and misses your team.

## Script A (Immediate)
Hey {{first_name}} — this is {{business_name}}. Sorry we missed your call. Want to get this handled today? Reply YES and we’ll text you next steps or send a booking link.

## Script B (15 min follow-up)
Quick follow-up from {{business_name}}. I can get you a quote/consult today. Want me to send available times?

## Script C (Last attempt)
Last message from {{business_name}} — still need help with {{service_type}}? If yes, reply 1 and we’ll prioritize you.

## If they reply YES
Perfect — here’s our fastest booking link: {{booking_link}}. If you prefer, reply with a good time and we’ll call you.
