# 06 — Objection Replies (Copy/Paste)

## “Too expensive”
Totally fair — most clients care about ROI. If we can show this pays for itself in recovered bookings, would you test it for 30 days?

## “Not interested”
No problem. Should we pause and check back in 30 days, or close this out completely?

## “Need to think about it”
Makes sense. What part do you want to think through — pricing, timing, or expected results?

## “Send info”
Absolutely — quick summary: this system recovers missed calls, stale leads, and no-shows into booked appointments.
